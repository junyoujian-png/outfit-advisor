概述

感謝您使用星座顧問。本隱私權政策說明我們如何收集、使用及保護您的資料。使用本 App 即表示您同意本政策所述內容。

最後更新：2026 年 4 月

收集的資料

本 App 僅收集以下最少量資料，且所有資料均儲存在您的裝置本機：

  • 語言設定：您選擇的介面語言（繁體中文或英文），儲存於裝置本機。
  • 星座選擇：您在 App 中選擇的星座，僅用於查詢當日運勢，不會傳送至任何伺服器。

我們不會收集您的姓名、電子郵件、位置、聯絡人或任何個人識別資訊。

廣告（Google AdMob）

本 App 使用 Google AdMob 展示廣告。AdMob 可能會依據您的裝置資訊（如廣告識別碼）提供個人化廣告。

Google AdMob

廣告服務由 Google LLC 提供。AdMob 的資料使用方式受 Google 隱私權政策規範。

查看 Google 隱私權政策

您可至裝置設定中重設廣告識別碼，或選擇退出個人化廣告。

第三方服務

本 App 使用以下第三方服務提供核心功能：

Supabase

用於儲存及提供每日星座運勢資料。Supabase 為雲端資料庫服務，您的星座查詢請求（僅含星座種類與日期）會傳送至 Supabase 伺服器。

查看 Supabase 隱私權政策

Groq AI

用於生成個人化穿搭建議。您輸入的穿搭相關資訊（如身高、體重、性別、星座、偏好）會傳送至 Groq AI 進行處理。請勿輸入任何敏感個人資料。

查看 Groq 隱私權政策

資料安全

我們不會販售、出租或交換您的個人資料給任何第三方。所有與外部服務的通訊均透過加密的 HTTPS 連線進行。

用戶權利

  • 您可隨時至裝置設定中清除 App 的本機資料（包含語言設定）。
  • 卸載本 App 即可刪除所有本機儲存的資料。
  • 如有任何疑問，請透過下方聯絡方式與我們聯繫。

兒童隱私

本 App 不針對 13 歲以下兒童設計，亦不會有意收集兒童的個人資料。

政策更新

我們可能不定期更新本隱私權政策。重大變更將於 App 內通知或於本頁面更新版本日期。

Overview

Thank you for using Zodiac Advisor. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal data necessary to provide the service. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Language preference: Your chosen interface language (Traditional Chinese or English), stored locally on your device.
  • Zodiac sign selection: The zodiac sign you select in the App, used only to fetch your daily horoscope. This is not associated with your identity.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or any personally identifiable information.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device information (such as an advertising identifier) to serve personalized ads.

Google AdMob

Advertising services are provided by Google LLC. AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

View Google's Privacy Policy

You may reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide core functionality:

Supabase

Used to store and serve daily horoscope data. Your horoscope requests (containing only zodiac type and date) are sent to Supabase servers.

View Supabase Privacy Policy

Groq AI

Used to generate personalized outfit recommendations. The outfit-related information you enter (e.g., height, weight, gender, zodiac sign, style preferences) is sent to Groq AI for processing. Please do not enter any sensitive personal information.

View Groq Privacy Policy

Data Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. All communications with external services are conducted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your Rights

  • You may clear the App's local data (including language preferences) at any time through your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.
  • For any concerns, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice or by updating the date on this page.
